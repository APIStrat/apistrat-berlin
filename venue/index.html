Venue

The Humboldt Carré, the Conference And Event Centre on Gendarmenmarkt

Address: Behrenstraße 42, 10117 Berlin

The Humboldt Carré it’s a house with history. Even at the time it was built in the 19th century, the Humboldt Carré was already an architectural masterpiece. So it’s not surprising that the combination of tradition and modernity is still convincing today.

 

The Humboldt Carré is located on the Berlin-Mitte neighborhood which represents a perfect location: Unter den Linden, Friedrichtstraße und Gendarmenmarkt just around the corner.
