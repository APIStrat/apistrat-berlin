About

APIDays Berlin and APIStrat Europe are joining forces!

APIs are starting to touch every industry on the planet and best practice is evolving faster and faster so the teams behind the APIDays Berlin and APIStrat conferences have combined to create a single new European Event - the conference will combine the best of both with strategy keynotes, in depth industry sessions, developer track and a by now infamous developer SpeedHack challenge. Come to Berlin in April and keep pace with the API World!
